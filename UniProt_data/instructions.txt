Instructions (general instructions are here: https://www.uniprot.org/help/retrieve_sets)
1.      Type “annotation:(type:act_site)” (without quotes) into the uniprot search bar
2.      At the top of the entries, click the button that says “Columns” with a pencil next to it
3.      Check “sequence” under sequences and “active site” and “binding site” under function
4.      Click save
5.      Click the Reviewed by Swiss-Prot button on the left
6.      Click the download button, select download all, tab format, uncompressed then click go
7.      Save the file as uniprot.tab somewhere of your choosing
The active sites/binding sites are saved as strings, see notebook for turning them into lists of just the site indices.
